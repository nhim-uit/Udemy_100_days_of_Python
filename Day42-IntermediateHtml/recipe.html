Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Combine the flour and salt, then add to the mixture.
  4. Knead the dough until smooth, then let rise for 1 hour.
  5. Roll out the dough, spread the filling, then roll up.
  6. Cut into slices, place in a pan, and let rise for 30 minutes.
  7. Bake at 350Â°F for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Enjoy your delicious cinnamon rolls!
